About the Learning Commons


The Learning Commons is available for all students on campus. It allows students who are falling behind to get free tutoring in classes they are struggling with. It also is a great way for students to get there grade up from a B to an A. All tutors at the Learning Commons have already taken the class and recieved an A in it.


Club Involvement


There are many clubs that Quinnipiac has to offer. With a student's email they can make an account on DoYouQU and see a list of all the organizations Quinnipiac has to offer. Some organizations that many software egineering majors are involved in are:

  • Quinnipiac University Engineering Student Organization (QUESO)
  • Quinnipiac Computing Club (QCC)
  • Quinnipiac Gaming Club
